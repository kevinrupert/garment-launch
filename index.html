You’ve changed your life.
Let us change your wardrobe.

The Garment Project is a non-profit that aims to empower women recovering from an eating disorder by providing them with new clothing individualized for their healthy bodies and lifestyles.

How it Works

The Garment Project receives new, never worn clothing donations from the same stores you already shop in.

We remove all tags and sizing information from each article of clothing ensuring that you can maintain your focused recovery.

We’ll send you the individualized website for you to browse and pick out things that you like.

We’ll pack up the items that you’ve picked out and ship them to you within a week. Decide what you want to keep and send back what doesn’t work.

The project

The Garment Project receives new, never worn clothing donations from the same stores you already shop in. We also use a portion of our monetary donations to purchase clothing items we think you’ll love as well.

Using individual personal information given to us by your treatment team, we will curate a shopping page just for you. Additionally, you’ll already be receiving a basics package with some essentials to get you started.
